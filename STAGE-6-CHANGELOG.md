# Stage 6 — Referrals and Mythic Achievements

- Personal family invitations are now available to every authenticated family member.
- Every invite stores the inviter and attributes successful joins to that profile.
- Added native Share support with clipboard fallback.
- Added hidden, collapsed referral statistics in the owner’s own profile.
- Tracks cumulative invited users, currently present referrals, referral XP, best streak, and gifts between referrals.
- Added eight referral achievements from “Перший друг” to “Легенда спільноти”.
- Added five Mythic achievements: “Архітектор долі”, “Нескінченність”, “Хранитель часу”, “Серце myHabbit”, and “Абсолют”.
- Referral achievements are granted server-side when a user joins and re-evaluated client-side.
