# myHabbit v6.0 — Stage 4

## Museum and permanent gifts

- Added a dedicated Museum section to the main navigation.
- Added museum statistics for opened stickers, completed collections, cosmetics, and achievements.
- Added permanent galleries for opened stickers and owned cosmetics.
- Added a gift station for transferring an owned sticker or cosmetic to another family member.
- Added optional postcard text up to 120 characters.
- Gift records store sender, recipient, item, date, and postcard forever in family history.
- Sticker gifts reduce the sender's inventory and increase the recipient's inventory.
- Cosmetic gifts transfer ownership and automatically unequip transferred cosmetics.
- Added +10 XP for sending a gift and +5 XP for receiving one.
- Added migration-safe giftHistory and receivedGifts fields for old profiles.
- Updated the PWA application version.
